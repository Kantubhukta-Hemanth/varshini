2NAU Pathway Program

The 2NAU pathway program was developed through partnerships between NAU and the Arizona community colleges. This program provides you with a seamless path to earn an affordable and respected NAU bachelor’s degree. You will begin your coursework at your community college, then transfer to NAU in Flagstaff, Online, or to one of our Statewide locations to finish your degree. We recommend signing up for this program at least two semesters before you are ready to transfer to maximize the benefits including a merit-based scholarship.
